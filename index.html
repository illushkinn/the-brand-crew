Rotisería · Mar del Plata

Luisito Playa Grande

View Luisito's site → Brandbook →

The problem: Luisito had a traditional rotisería in Mar del Plata — good food, loyal customers, but no online presence. People who didn't walk by his door never knew what he was making.

What we built: A digital menu with online ordering system, brand identity from scratch, and a website that feels like walking into his shop. Connected to WhatsApp so every order arrives as a message.

Why it works: Because the food speaks first. One client tried the spinach tarts and left this review — unprompted, real, and better than anything we could write:

«Las mejores tartas de espinaca están acá. Son como un alfajor de espinaca y pascualina, literal. Gorditas, explotadas de espinaca, con un toque de huevo cocido. Me encantan estas tartas. Ah, sí: por supuesto me atendieron muy bien.»

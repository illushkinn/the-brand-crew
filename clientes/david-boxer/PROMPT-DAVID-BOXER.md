# Prompt Context — David Kovchenko Boxing Landing

## PROMPT TO GENERATE A PERSONAL BRAND LANDING PAGE FOR DAVID (BOXER)

```
CONTEXT & STRATEGY:
David Kovchenko is a 22-year-old Ukrainian-Argentinian boxer from Mar del Plata (Alfar area), Argentina. He is a talented fighter — blonde, lean but muscular build, attractive facial features. He trains regularly and has photos/videos of himself boxing, hitting the bag, doing mitt work, and strength training.

THE BUSINESS MODEL (important):
David is NOT a retailer. He is an ATHLETE + INFLUENCER. His landing page serves as:

1. Personal brand hub — "David Kovchenko Boxing"
2. Product showcase — boxing gear he personally uses and endorses
3. Affiliate/partnership hub — brands send him products, he reviews them, earns commission
4. Future: his own training programs / e-books via Hotmart

HOW IT WORKS:
- Boxing brands send David FREE products (gloves, wraps, bags, apparel)
- He tests them, records himself training with them
- Posts on Instagram/TikTok + the landing page
- Fans buy through his affiliate/Hotmart links
- David earns commission, brands get authentic content

THE AUDIENCE:
- Young boxers, fighters, gym-goers in Argentina and LATAM (16-35)
- People who follow boxing content on Instagram/TikTok
- Spanish-speaking, mobile-first
- Looking for quality gear endorsed by a real fighter

REFERENCE DESIGN — JUJIMUFU.COM:
The design should follow the jujimufu model:
- Clean, minimalist aesthetic (NOT dark/heavy)
- White or light background
- Brand is the ATHLETE, not the products
- Large hero with David's photo as the focal point
- Simple product grid with photos of HIM wearing/using each item
- Prices visible, no login required
- Newsletter signup at the bottom (low friction)
- Social links (Instagram, YouTube, TikTok)

PERSONAL BRAND NAMING:
- "David Kovchenko Boxing"
- Or simply "David Kovchenko"
- Tagline idea: "Boxeo real. Productos probados."

PAGE STRUCTURE:

1. NAVBAR (minimal)
   - Logo: "DAVID KOVCHENKO" (text or simple wordmark)
   - Links: Products · About · Contact
   - Instagram icon (most important social)

2. HERO
   - Full-width photo of David in action (boxing pose, punching bag, or portrait)
   - Overlay text: "DAVID KOVCHENKO"
   - Subtitle: "Boxeador. Atleta. Probando el mejor equipo para vos."
   - CTA: "Ver productos que uso"

3. FEATURED PRODUCTS (grid)
   - 2-4 columns responsive (2 on mobile, 3-4 on desktop)
   - Each card: product photo (David wearing/using it) → product name → price → "Comprar" button (Hotmart link)
   - Pricing badges: "Envío gratis" or "Recomendado"
   - Products he currently endorses (gloves, hand wraps, bag, etc.)

4. ABOUT DAVID (short, authentic)
   - "Tengo 22 años, boxeo desde los [X]. También soy mecánico de motos en Alfar, MDP."
   - "No vendo lo que no uso. Cada producto en esta página lo probé en el gym."
   - Photo of David (training or casual)

5. INSTAGRAM FEED / SOCIAL PROOF
   - Embeds or gallery of his best Instagram posts training
   - "Seguime en Instagram para ver mis entrenamientos"
   - Follow button

6. NEWSLETTER (simple, like jujimufu)
   - "Sumate a mi lista. Tips de entrenamiento, productos nuevos, y sorteos."
   - Single field: email → Subscribe button

7. CONTACT / PARTNERSHIPS
   - "¿Sos una marca de boxeo? Trabajemos juntos."
   - Email or WhatsApp contact
   - Brief: "Probá tus productos con un boxeador real. Contenido auténtico para LATAM."

8. FOOTER
   - Social icons (Instagram, YouTube, TikTok)
   - Hotmart badge
   - "© 2026 David Kovchenko"

KEY FEATURES NEEDED:
- Mobile responsive (100%)
- WhatsApp floating button (click-to-chat)
- Hotmart/affiliate buttons on each product
- Fast loading under 2s
- SEO meta tags (og:image, description, etc.)
- Instagram integration (feed or link)
- No shopping cart needed — each product links externally to Hotmart
- Light/clean aesthetic (not dark mode)

TECH STACK:
- Static site (Vercel or Netlify = free)
- HTML + CSS + vanilla JS
- No backend needed
- Future: n8n for newsletter automation

CONTENT NEEDED FROM DAVID:
- 3-5 high quality photos of him training/posing
- 1 hero photo (full body, action)
- List of products he currently uses and endorses
- Instagram/TikTok handle
- Brands he'd like to partner with

THE ASK:
Generate a complete single-page landing with:
- index.html (responsive, light aesthetic, jujimufu-inspired)
- CSS (clean, minimalist, athlete-first)
- JS (smooth scroll, WhatsApp button, newsletter form)
- Hotmart buy buttons integration
- Placeholder images for David's photos
```
